About

I’m MAKENGA Kaziyabwana and I have a passion for Lake Kivu and all it has to offer. Lake Kivu is in Rwanda's Karongi district of Kibuye. It’s a beautiful place that is full of unique adventures. I’ve lived here my entire life and am a licensed and experienced captain and tour guide. I own two well-equipped safe boats and I love to take visitors around the lake and the islands that I love and am proud to call home. You can contact me on WhatsApp to make a reservation.
